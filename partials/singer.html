Vocalist

Singing since a child, my debut was when I was 11 years old at my sister's wedding. That was the start of a life-long love of music and singing. Growing up singing in our local church and school functions has given me a rich foundation of theory and genres along with 9 years of piano lessons. I have had the honor of singing in a choir in Europe, directing a Gospel choir during college and instructing teens when I was a highschool choir director.

Since moving to Nashville, I have worked with talented producers, songwriters and musicians. In 2009/2010 I was listed as one of the top 5 demo singers on demosinger.com. With a two-octave range, from (low D to D above high C), my lead vocals have colorful emotion and can handle most any genre. I have been told I sound like Sara Evans, Patty Lovelass, Trisha Yearwood and Diana Krall.

Genres (include, but not limited to): Country (New and Old), Pop, Blues, Rock, Contemporary Christian, Gospel, Jazz

Located in the Greater Nashville area, I am available to lend my lead/harmony vocals with short notice. Using the Nashville Numbers System, I can quickly learn songs and save time in the studio.

Feel free to text/call/email to learn more. The best email to reach me about music projects is debragmusic@gmail.com
